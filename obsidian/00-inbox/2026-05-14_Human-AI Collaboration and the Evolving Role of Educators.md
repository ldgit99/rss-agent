## Abstract
The increase of intelligent technologies in learning environments reshapes how knowledge is created, shared, and guided. Rather than replacing humans, these systems are utilized as collaborators that support personalization, feedback, and decision-making in educational contexts. This shift invites educators to move toward facilitation, mentorship, and ethical use of learning technologies. As human insight and machine capabilities intersect, teaching practices can be redefined to better support creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. "Human-AI Collaboration and the Evolving Role of Educators" explores digital education technologies and AI for e-learning environments. It examines how AI enhances teaching, learning, curriculum development, and institutional decision-making. This book covers topics such as educational technologies, instructional design, and professional development, and is a useful resource for educators, engineers, academicians, researchers, and scientists.
