## Abstract
The rapid integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into higher education is reshaping how future educators are prepared for multilingual, multicultural, and ethically complex environments. This study employed a mixed-methods sequential explanatory design to investigate how educators and students in Luxembourg and Ukraine perceive and adopt AI in teacher preparation. In Luxembourg, AI was largely framed as a tool to extend inclusive, multilingual practices and institutional innovation, whereas in Ukraine, it was often adopted out of necessity under conditions of crisis and infrastructural disruption. Quantitative analyses demonstrated that perceived benefits of AI were positively correlated with readiness for instructional integration (r = 0.61, p &lt; .001), while ethical concerns were negatively correlated with willingness to adopt AI (r = –0.45, p &lt; .001). No significant group differences were observed in overall reported levels of ethical concern (t(140) = 1.25, p = .21), though qualitative findings revealed striking contrasts in the nature of these concerns. Luxembourgish participants expressed preventative concerns about quality and pedagogy, while Ukrainian participants described immediate ethical dilemmas shaped by wartime conditions, including studying in bomb shelters and balancing personal safety with academic integrity. Findings highlight that while AI can enhance instructional innovation, it simultaneously amplifies inequalities through language bias and access gaps. The study contributes by linking empirical insights to instructional design frameworks (ADDIE, TPACK) and multicultural education theory, offering concrete guidance for teacher preparation in both stable and crisis-affected contexts. # Summary
본 연구는 인공지능(AI)의 고등 교육 통합이 미래 교육자 준비에 미치는 영향을 조사하였다. 룩셈부르크와 우크라이나의 교육자와 학생들이 AI를 어떻게 인식하고 채택하는지를 분석하기 위해 혼합 방법 연구를 수행하였다. 연구 결과, AI의 인식된 이점은 교수 통합 준비성과 긍정적인 상관관계를 보였으며(r = 0.61, p < .001), 윤리적 우려는 AI 채택 의지와 부정적인 상관관계를 나타냈다(r = -0.45, p < .001). 룩셈부르크 참가자들은 품질과 교수법에 대한 예방적 우려를 표현한 반면, 우크라이나 참가자들은 전쟁 상황에서의 즉각적인 윤리적 딜레마를 설명하였다. 연구 결과는 AI가 교수 혁신을 향상시킬 수 있지만, 언어 편향과 접근성 격차를 통해 불평등을 심화시킬 수 있음을 강조한다.

## Problem

## Design
혼합 방법 연구로 정량적 설문조사와 정성적 인터뷰를 결합.

## Findings
AI의 이점은 교수 통합 준비성과 긍정적 상관관계, 윤리적 우려는 AI 채택 의지와 부정적 상관관계.

## Conclusion
AI는 교육 혁신을 촉진할 수 있지만, 불평등을 심화시킬 수 있는 잠재력을 지님.
