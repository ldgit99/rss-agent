## Abstract
The rapid emergence of artificial intelligence (AI) in education underscores the imperative to equip future educators with the competencies needed to meaningfully integrate AI into instruction. Hence, this study aimed to effectively cultivate Technological Pedagogical Content Knowledge for AI (AI-TPACK) competence among preservice science teachers (PSSTs) through the implementation of an AI-focused pedagogical learning course. Anchored on social constructivist principles, the course integrated structured instruction, hands-on activities, collaborative lesson design, and reflective practices to enhance both PSSTs’ understanding and practical application of AI in science education. A pre-experimental one-group pretest-posttest design was employed with 84 PSSTs enrolled in a state university in the Philippines. Quantitative data were collected using an adapted AI-TPACK scale, while qualitative insights were gathered through structured interviews. Non-parametric analysis using the Wilcoxon Signed-Ranks Test revealed statistically significant improvements across all dimensions of AI-TPACK, namely technological knowledge, pedagogical applications, ethical considerations, and integrated competence (z = -6.900, p &lt; .001, r = 0.76), indicating a large effect size. Thematic analysis of PSSTs’ reflections identified key affordances such as enhanced pedagogical design, improved AI literacy, and increased student engagement, alongside constraints related to tool limitations, ethical dilemmas, and contextual barriers. The findings highlight the potential of strategically designed teacher education interventions in fostering AI-TPACK competence. The study contributes empirical evidence and pedagogical insights for advancing AI integration in pre- and in-service teacher training, reinforcing the need for intentional design, policy alignment, and continued research on equitable and context-sensitive AI adaption in science education.

# Summary
본 연구는 인공지능(AI)의 교육적 통합을 위한 예비 과학교사(PSSTs)의 기술적 교수 내용 지식(AI-TPACK) 역량을 개발하기 위한 AI 중심의 교수 학습 과정을 설계하고 실행하였다. 사회 구성주의 원리에 기반하여, 구조화된 수업, 실습 활동, 협력적 수업 설계 및 반성적 실천을 통합하여 PSSTs의 AI 이해 및 적용 능력을 향상시켰다. 84명의 PSSTs를 대상으로 한 사전-사후 실험 설계에서, AI-TPACK 척도를 사용한 양적 데이터와 구조화된 인터뷰를 통한 질적 데이터를 수집하였다. 비모수 분석 결과, AI-TPACK의 모든 차원에서 통계적으로 유의미한 개선이 나타났으며(z = -6.900, p < .001, r = 0.76), 이는 큰 효과 크기를 나타낸다. PSSTs의 반성적 분석을 통해 교수 설계 향상, AI 리터러시 개선, 학생 참여 증가 등의 주요 장점이 확인되었으며, 도구의 한계, 윤리적 딜레마, 맥락적 장벽과 같은 제약도 나타났다. 연구 결과는 AI-TPACK 역량을 촉진하기 위한 전략적 교사 교육 개입의 잠재력을 강조한다.
